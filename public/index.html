YDWC is a network and hub for young professionals from diverse and underrepresented backgrounds.

We believe in the power of community. We work to empower our members with the resources, connections and knowledge to drive professional and personal development.

Our mission is to connect young, talented professionals, with genuinely life changing opportunities to advance their careers and create positive social change within their communities.

Our Values

Knowledge Network

Our highly emmersive and engaging range of events offer endless opportunities to connect with a diverse range of young professionals to exchange experiences and insights into different professional industries and professional development routes

Talent Pool

Surrounding yourself with people who will inspire and uplift you, is fundamental to your level of attainment in life. Our mission is to create a platform to facilitate high value exchanges of ideas, perspectives and ambitions between the brightest talents from underrepresented background across the UK

Community Relationships

Where we fall short as individuals, we rise as a community. At YDWC we pride ourselves on creating a welcoming and inclusive environment to facilitate the development of long-lasting relationships. Our network is a space to meet new people and to connect with peers that we often find ourselves alienated from as we progress professionally

Support Network

We offer a range of consultation services to provide support and advice throughout each step of your early career progression. Through partnerships with a number of FTSE 350 companies we are able to provide invaluable insights and opportunities to help develop your career, and drive your personal development

Changing Societal Narratives

Societal narratives influence how diverse professionals are percieved within society and corporate culture; who they are and the value they contribute. At YDWC we aim to redefine societal narratives through positive social action, to reflect the true essence of being a young professional from underrepresented backgrounds in the modern age.
